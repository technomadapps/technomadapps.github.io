Expand your search capability.

Search for multiple places or services in one go. Check the different results on the map simultaneously, plan a multi-step route, and start navigating. 

Are you looking for ATMs, bakeries, gas stations around you or everywhere, and you want to see all of them on one map at the same time? 

With Multisearch Map, you can.

  • Set the location and radius for your search
  • Add one or more keywords [to your search] and customise their color indicator
  • Enable Deep Search mode to get more results
  • Add places to your navigation route or pin them
  • Start Google Maps Navigation with the multi-step route you have planned
  • Save and rename searches that you use often or delete the ones you don’t need anymore.

Multisearch Map is a helpful tool to:

  • Show different types of services simultaneously on the map
  • Travel more efficiently around places you don’t know well
  • Plan and start a multi-step route navigation

Enjoy your multi-search!
