Explore and compare maps in a new way

Sometimes you need just more than one map.

Improve your view of the earth's surface. Explore your tracks with more detail, simulate an airplane view more efficiently, compare and mark areas, play!

Two maps side by side or overlapping each other, of the same or different type (street/satellite/terrain), you choose.

Maybe you want to compare the size and shape of different areas visually, merge the details of a street and satellite view together, or check a point from different perspectives, and so on.

2Maps, here we go! 

  • Center your maps in the same or in two different locations
  • Select and move your maps separately or simultaneously with a single touch
  • Set zoom, tilt, bearing, map type for each map or synchronise these values between them
  • Draw lines to mark areas on just one or both maps simultaneously
  • Transpose the two maps with the full screen mode and use the transparency tool to quickly switch from street to satellite view
  • Enable the "Use true size" setting and compare areas considering the size distortion with latitudes
  • Save the maps to quickly reload their content later
  • Rename or delete your saved maps.
