The location-based alarm system.

Are you always on the move and need to be notified when entering or leaving specific areas?

Do you want to take a nap on your bus or train without missing your stop?

Do you want to be reminded to buy anything when approaching your favourite stores?

With a clear and intuitive UI, create, visualise, and manage your location-based alarms with GPS Alarm.

  • Pin point on the map where you want your alarm to be triggered and set a radius
  • Complete your alarm with title, message, and the type of transition (entering, exiting, or both)
  • Choose whether to be notified with a standard notification, your default ringtone or alarm
  • Enable or disable single alarms or use the main switch.
